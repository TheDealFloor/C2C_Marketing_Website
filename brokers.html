Why It Works

Qualified buyers. No tire-kickers.

Every buyer on the C2C platform has been reviewed and approved. They've submitted proof of funds, defined their buy box, and signed the NDA. When your deal hits the platform, you're not broadcasting to the public — you're serving it directly to the people most likely to make an offer.

The result is fewer wasted conversations, faster offers, and transactions that actually close — without your relationships getting exposed or bypassed.

How It Works

Simple to submit. Structured to close.

Register & Get Approved

Create your broker profile on the platform. Our team reviews your application and gets you set up — typically within one business day.

Submit Your Property

Upload your offering memorandum, rent roll, T12, and photos. Our team reviews the deal, runs internal underwriting, and prepares it for distribution. Broker information is protected — buyers see the deal, not how to contact you directly.

Deal Goes Live on the Platform

Your listing is matched to approved buyers whose buy box fits and distributed via the platform dashboard and direct email. Activity — views, interest, questions — is tracked in real time.

Offers Come In

Interested buyers submit soft LOIs through the platform. Our team forwards qualified offers and coordinates the next steps with you directly.

Close the Deal

From LOI acceptance through contract and closing, every step flows through the C2C system — documented, tracked, and protected.
