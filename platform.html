What We Built

Not a listing site.
A private marketplace.

Most commercial real estate platforms are open directories. Anyone can browse. Deals sit in public view. Relationships get circumvented. That's not how serious capital moves.

Coast 2 Coast Properties is different — a closed, invitation-grade system where every member is verified, every deal is curated, and every step of the transaction is tracked and protected inside our ecosystem.

The result: deals that actually close, with buyers who are actually ready, and relationships that stay intact.

Deal flow, not noise

Deals are curated before they hit the platform. No stale listings, no tire-kickers — every property is reviewed, underwritten, and matched to buyers whose criteria fit.

The ecosystem stays closed

Buyers, brokers, and connectors all operate within the C2C platform. Non-circumvention is built in — not bolted on.

Move fast when it matters

From NDA to offer to executed LOI, the platform removes every unnecessary step. When a deal fits, buyers act in minutes, not days.

How It Works

From first look to closed deal — every step is protected.

1

Register & Sign the NDA

Create your member profile and execute the Non-Circumvention / Non-Disclosure Agreement. This unlocks deal teasers and the platform dashboard — and protects everyone in the room.

2

Submit Proof of Funds & Buyer Bio

To unlock full deal rooms — financials, rent rolls, documents — you verify capital capacity and submit a short buyer bio. Our team reviews and approves. Serious buyers only.

3

Get Matched to Deals

Once approved, you are matched to active deals that fit your buy box — geography, asset class, price range, and return profile. Deals come to you.

4

Submit Your Offer

When a deal fits, submit a soft LOI directly through the platform. Pre-filled. Ready to sign. One click from interest to executed offer.

5

Close the Deal

Our team coordinates through LOI acceptance, contract, EMD, and closing — every document tracked inside the platform. Coast to coast, start to finish.
