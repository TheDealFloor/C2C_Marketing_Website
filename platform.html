What We Built

Not a listing site.
A private marketplace.

Most commercial real estate platforms are open directories. Anyone can browse. Deals sit in public view. Relationships get circumvented. That's not how serious capital moves.

Coast 2 Coast Properties is different. It's a closed, invitation-grade system — every member is verified, every deal is curated, and every step of the transaction is tracked and protected inside our ecosystem.

The result: deals that actually close, with buyers who are actually ready, and relationships that stay intact.

How It Works

From first look to closed deal — every step is protected.

Register & Sign the NDA

Create your member profile and execute the Non-Circumvention / Non-Disclosure Agreement. This unlocks access to deal teasers and the platform dashboard — and protects everyone in the room.

Submit Proof of Funds & Buyer Bio

To unlock full deal rooms — financials, rent rolls, documents — you verify your capital capacity and submit a short buyer bio. Our team reviews and approves. Serious buyers only.

Get Matched to Deals

Once approved, you're matched to active deals that fit your buy box — geography, asset class, price range, and return profile. Deals come to you, not the other way around.

Submit Your Offer

When a deal fits, submit a soft LOI directly through the platform. Pre-filled. Ready to sign. One click to move from interest to executed offer.

Close the Deal

Our team coordinates through LOI acceptance, contract, EMD, and closing — with every document tracked inside the platform. Coast to coast, start to finish.
