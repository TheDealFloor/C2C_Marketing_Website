How to Get Access

Three steps from registration to deal flow.

The approval process is fast. We're not trying to create friction — we're making sure everyone on the platform belongs there.

Register & Sign the NDA

Create your account and execute the Non-Circumvention / Non-Disclosure Agreement. This gives you access to deal teasers and the buyer dashboard.

Submit POF & Buyer Bio

Upload your proof of funds and a short buyer profile — asset class preferences, geography, typical deal size. Our team reviews and approves qualified buyers.

Receive Matched Deals

Once approved, deals matching your buy box arrive directly — email notifications, platform dashboard, or both. Review, analyze, and submit offers without leaving C2C.
